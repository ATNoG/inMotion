\section{Related Work}

Passenger counting spans multiple sensing modalities. Video-based APC using YOLOv5 achieves high accuracy but degrades under occlusion and variable lighting~\cite{pronello2023evaluating}. Edge AI cameras address scalability~\cite{ono2025realtime}. ML pipelines on traditional APC data enable offline correction of sensor errors~\cite{wiboonsiriruk2023efficient}.

Wi-Fi sensing avoids the cost and privacy drawbacks of cameras. iABACUS~\cite{nitti2020iabacus} demonstrated counting via Wi-Fi probe requests. Myrvoll et al.~\cite{myrvoll2017counting} used Wi-Fi signature counts for ridership estimation. Fabre et al.~\cite{fabre2025machine} applied random forests and gradient boosting to Wi-Fi sensor data. Simoncic et al.~\cite{simoncic2023nonintrusive} monitored presence through passive probe requests. CSI-based methods~\cite{wang2024csi} offer finer signal characterization but require specialized hardware unavailable on commodity access points. RSSI fingerprinting for localization has been extensively surveyed~\cite{singh2021rssisurvey}.

Recent advances in sequence modeling inform our architectural choices. Mamba~\cite{gu2023mamba} introduced selective state-space models with $O(L)$ complexity. Mamba-3 MIMO~\cite{gu2026mamba3} extended this paradigm with multi-input multi-output capabilities. Autoformer~\cite{wu2021autoformer} replaced self-attention with FFT-based auto-correlation mechanisms. Mixture of Experts~\cite{shazeer2017moe} enables conditional computation through sparse activation.

Our prior work~\cite{ribeiro2026eucnc} established classical ML baselines on this dataset. Across 34 algorithms, Gaussian Processes achieved the best combined MCC of 0.756. This paper extends that analysis with 11 \ac{DL} architectures, comprehensive \ac{HPO} via Optuna~\cite{optuna2019}, and systematic interference analysis.
